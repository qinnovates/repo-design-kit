## How to Apply

1. **Pick a skin** from above
2. **Copy the SVG gradient stops** into your `docs/assets/header-dark.svg` and `header-light.svg`
3. **Copy the Mermaid style block** and paste it at the bottom of every Mermaid diagram in your README
4. **Use Neo-palette hex codes** in your shields.io badge URLs: `?color=39FF14`

Everything matches. Header, diagrams, badges — one visual system.
